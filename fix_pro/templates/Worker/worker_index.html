Who can register on the platform?

Industries, suppliers, and users related to the marine sector can create accounts. Administrators verify and approve registrations for secure access.

Can I track my orders online?

Yes, the platform provides real-time order tracking, allowing users to monitor the status of their purchases from confirmation to delivery.

Are all products verified for quality?

Absolutely. All vendors are verified and their products are categorized to maintain quality standards for fisheries, shipping, research, and tourism.

Can I give feedback or rate products?

Yes, users can submit feedback and ratings for products they purchase, helping maintain transparency and guide other buyers.
