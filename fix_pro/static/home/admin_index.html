Key Features of FixPro

A smart platform designed to connect users with trusted service professionals.

Multi-Role Access

Supports Admin, Workers, and Users with secure login and role-based access for smooth platform management.

Verified Professionals

All service providers are verified and approved by administrators to ensure reliability, safety, and service quality.

Support & Complaints

Integrated complaint and feedback system enables fast communication and issue resolution between users, workers, and admins.

Quick Booking

Book skilled professionals instantly based on service type, availability, and location with real-time updates.

Secure Payments

Safe and transparent payment processing after service completion, ensuring trust for both users and workers.

Ratings & Reviews

Users can rate and review services, helping maintain high-quality standards and guide future customers.
